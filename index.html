Milk Tea Recipe

Tea Expert Vikram and Chef Nishant tell us How to Make Tea,
While also giving us tips on Tea Brewing Mistakes that one can avoid to get that perfect cup of Steaming Deliciousness.

Total Time : 10 Minutes

Ingredients Required

  • 1 Cup (250 ml) Milk
  • 2 TeaSpoons Tea Powder
  • 1/4 Cup Water (Approx. 60 ml)
  • 3 TeaSpoons Sugar

Preparation Strategy

  1. Boil Water in a Saucepan.

    Time : 1 Minutes

  2. Add Sugar and Tea Powder in it and Boil it for 3 to 4 Minutes on Medium Flame.

    Time : 4 Minutes

  3. Add Milk and Boil it over Medium Flame for 5 to 6 Minutes or until Bubble Starts to Rise.
    You will see the change in the Color of the Tea from Milky Shade to Brown Shade when it is Ready.

    Time : 5 Minutes
